Batrade

Trade like a Bat!

All platform components, including the backend, frontend, key management, and more, will be completely open source. Nothing will be proprietary or closed.

Pure and simple, just trading, no distractions. Built for users of all experience levels.

Only real humans and manual traders. No bots, No automated trading.

Listings are open and external. Any blockchain can be listed by providing at least 16 random nodes, a secure key generation algorithm, and the ability to sign and broadcast deposit and withdrawal transactions. All listing information will be fully open source and auditable by the community. No fees involved.

All deposit and withdrawal transactions, including hot and cold wallets for all assets, will be open, public, and available for users to monitor and trace.

All trades will be public, including seller and buyer information. Each user's trade history will remain public for one month to prevent misconduct. Users are rated based on their activity.

Users can generate a web wallet while managing their own wallet seed. We manage their assets. The wallet can be used in offline mode to enhance security.

Each user has their own social networking page and can follow other traders, share news, and trade updates with each other.
